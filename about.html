Our Story

Welcome to Chai Cafe!

Welcome to Chai Cafe, a recognized brand in Sri Lanka, where we have been serving the finest coffee and providing unparalleled customer service for over two decades. Started 20 years ago, we have always strived to provide our customers with naturally selected, freshly roasted, high quality and high standard coffee products. We ensure that every cup of coffee we brew is crafted with utmost care and precision. With an impressive network of outlets across the island, we have won the love of the community across the country. We ensure that no matter where you are in Sri Lanka, you can enjoy a perfectly brewed cup of coffee.

At Chai Cafe, our skilled baristas undergo rigorous training to master the art of creating the perfect cup of coffee. We ensure that every sip of coffee they brew is a delightful experience. We also create unforgettable moments for our guests and provide exceptional customer service.

Whether you need a quiet place to study, a quick pick-me-up on the way to work, a cozy place to meet up with friends, or a serene place to enjoy a quiet moment alone, Chai Cafe is your perfect second home. Come in and experience the warmth of true coffee culture.
